Potato Soup

A Creamy and Delicious Recipe

This bowl of potato soup is sure to warm your heart and soul. An easy recipe topped off with green onions, bacon bits, and cheddar cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 Lb White Potatoes
  • 1 Large Onion
  • 1 24 Oz. carton of chicken broth
  • 1 12 Oz. Box of Dehydrated Potatoes
  • 1 T of Seasoned Salt
  • A pinch of Red Pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut the potatoes into bite sized cubes
  2. Dice the onion and add to the pot with butter. Sautee until soft.
  3. Add the potatoes and cook until the begin to sweat.
